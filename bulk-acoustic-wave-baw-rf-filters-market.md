<p><ul><li>FBAR</li><li>SMR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) RF Filters market is categorized into two types: Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator (FBAR) and Solid Mounted Resonator (SMR). FBAR technology utilizes a thin film of piezoelectric material sandwiched between two electrodes to generate acoustic waves. It offers higher performance in terms of low insertion loss and high selectivity. On the other hand, SMR technology involves the use of a solid material resonator, which allows for cost-effective manufacturing and integration. Both FBAR and SMR are widely used in various applications requiring RF signal filtering.</p></p>
