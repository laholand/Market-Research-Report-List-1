<p><strong>The Micro Motor System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brush</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micro Motor System market includes two main types: Brush and Brushless motors. Brush motors consist of a rotating armature wrapped with a coil that interacts with stationary magnets, creating electromagnetic forces to produce motion. They are affordable but have limited lifespan due to the wear of brushes. On the other hand, Brushless motors use a permanent magnet rotor and stationary coils, eliminating the need for brushes. They offer higher efficiency, durability, and require less maintenance, but are generally more expensive.</p></p>
