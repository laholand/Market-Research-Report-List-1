<p><ul><li>Bottom Loading Arms</li><li>Top Loading Arms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LNG truck loading arm market consists of two main types: bottom loading arms and top loading arms. Bottom loading arms are used to load LNG from underground storage tanks into tanker trucks. They are typically installed above the truck, allowing for safe and efficient transfer of the liquefied gas. On the other hand, top loading arms are used to load LNG from overhead storage tanks into tanker trucks. They are positioned on top of the truck, providing cost-effective and reliable loading solutions for transportation of LNG.</p></p>
