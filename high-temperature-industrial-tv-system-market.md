<p><p>The High Temperature Industrial TV System Market comprises two major types of sensors - CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensor and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensor. CCD sensors use a complex structure to convert light into electric charges, offering high-quality images with low noise levels. CMOS sensors, on the other hand, have individual pixels that convert light into electric signals, resulting in lower power consumption and faster data processing. Both sensors play a crucial role in capturing and transmitting high-temperature industrial television images, but they differ in their technology and performance characteristics.</p></p>
