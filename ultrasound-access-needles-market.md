<p><strong>The Ultrasound Access Needles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EUS-FNA</li><li>EUS-FNB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasound Access Needles Market includes two types: EUS-FNA and EUS-FNB. EUS-FNA (endoscopic ultrasound-guided fine needle aspiration) involves inserting a thin needle through an endoscope to collect tissue samples for examination. It is commonly used for diagnosing and staging cancers. On the other hand, EUS-FNB (endoscopic ultrasound-guided fine needle biopsy) utilizes a larger needle to obtain larger tissue samples, improving diagnostic accuracy. Both techniques are valuable in minimally invasive procedures and play a significant role in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions.</p></p>
