<p><p>Large-area image sensors are used in various applications including digital cameras, smartphones, and surveillance systems. The two main types of image sensors in the market are CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor) and CCD (charge-coupled device) sensors. CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in consumer electronics. On the other hand, CCD sensors offer higher image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for applications where image accuracy is critical such as scientific imaging and professional photography.</p></p>
