<p><p>PERS (Personal Emergency Response Systems) Devices are designed to help individuals seek immediate assistance during emergencies. There are three main market types for PERS devices: </p><p>1. Mobile Type: These devices are portable and can be carried by users wherever they go. They often feature GPS technology to track the user's location and enable quick response in case of emergencies outside the home.</p><p>2. Landline Type: These devices are connected to a landline telephone system. They allow users to call for help by pressing a button on the device, which connects them to a monitoring center or designated contacts.</p><p>3. Standalone Type: These devices operate independently without the need for additional connections. They are typically installed in a fixed location, like a home, and provide a direct means to contact emergency services or designated contacts during an emergency.</p></p>
