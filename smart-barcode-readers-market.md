<p><p>The Smart Barcode Readers Market consists of two major types: handheld barcode readers and stationary barcode readers. Handheld barcode readers are portable and can be easily carried around to scan barcodes on various products. They often require a manual trigger to activate the scanning process. On the other hand, stationary barcode readers are fixed in one place, such as at a checkout counter, and automatically scan barcodes as products pass by. They are commonly used in retail and logistics industries for efficient inventory management and tracking.</p></p>
