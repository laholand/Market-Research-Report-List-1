<p><p>The music headphone market is classified into three types: wired, wireless, and Bluetooth. Wired headphones use a physical cable to connect to the audio device, ensuring a reliable and uninterrupted audio signal. Wireless headphones, on the other hand, utilize radio frequency or infrared to transmit audio wirelessly, offering freedom of movement. Bluetooth headphones specifically use Bluetooth technology to connect to devices, allowing for convenient wireless streaming. Each type has its advantages, with wired offering stability, wireless providing mobility, and Bluetooth combining mobility with ease of use.</p></p>
