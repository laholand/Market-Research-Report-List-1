<p><p>The squirrel cage induction motor is the most widely used type in the three-phase AC asynchronous motor market. It is characterized by its simplicity and low cost, making it suitable for various applications such as pumps, fans, and compressors. On the other hand, the wound rotor induction motor offers variable speed control and high starting torque, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications such as cranes and mills.
