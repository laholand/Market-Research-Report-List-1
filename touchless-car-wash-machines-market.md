<p><strong>The Touchless Car Wash Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tunnel Type</li><li>Rollover Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touchless car wash machines market encompasses two main types: tunnel type and rollover type. Tunnel type machines are generally larger and are used in automated car wash facilities. Cars are driven through a tunnel-like structure where multiple nozzles spray water, detergent, and other cleaning agents onto the vehicle. In contrast, rollover type machines are smaller and are typically found in self-service car washes. Cars remain stationary while the machine moves over them, spraying water and cleaning solutions to wash the vehicle. Both types offer automated and efficient car washing methods.</p></p>
