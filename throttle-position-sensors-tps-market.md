<p><p>Throttle Position Sensors (TPS) are electronic devices used in vehicles to detect and measure the position of the throttle valve. There are two main types of TPS available in the market: End Switches Type and Potentiometer Type. End Switches Type sensors provide feedback on the fully closed and fully open positions of the throttle valve, allowing the vehicle's control unit to adjust the fuel injection accordingly. Potentiometer Type sensors use a resistive element to measure the throttle valve position continuously, providing smooth and accurate data for the control unit to manage engine performance.</p></p>
