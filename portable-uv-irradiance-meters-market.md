<p><p>Portable UV irradiance meters are devices used to measure the intensity of ultraviolet (UV) radiation in various settings. The market for these meters can be categorized into three types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C irradiance meters. UV-A meters measure the intensity of long-wave UVA radiation, which is primarily responsible for skin tanning and aging. UV-B meters measure the intensity of mid-range UVB radiation, which can cause sunburn and skin cancer. UV-C meters measure the intensity of short-wave UVC radiation, which is germicidal and often used for disinfection purposes, such as in healthcare settings.</p></p>
