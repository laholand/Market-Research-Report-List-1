<p><strong>The Electric Vacuum Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Leaf Type</li><li>Swing Piston Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric vacuum pump market is segmented into three main types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. The diaphragm type operates by flexing a diaphragm to create suction and is known for its compact size and low noise. The leaf type uses a series of interconnected leaves to create a vacuum, offering high efficiency and a small footprint. The swing piston type works by the reciprocating motion of a piston, delivering strong suction power. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
