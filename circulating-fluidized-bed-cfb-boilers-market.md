<p><p>Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) Boilers market can be categorized into subcritical, supercritical, and ultra-supercritical types. Subcritical boilers operate at lower steam pressure and are cost-effective. Supercritical boilers operate at higher temperatures and pressures, resulting in greater efficiency and reduced emissions. Ultra-supercritical boilers operate at even higher temperatures and pressures, offering the highest efficiency levels and lowest emissions.
