<p><p>A concentric ring water bath is a laboratory instrument used for heating substances at a controlled temperature. It consists of a stainless-steel container with one or more concentric rings that hold containers of different sizes. The bath is filled with water, and a heating element located at the bottom of the bath raises the temperature to the desired level. The concentric rings provide stability and allow multiple samples to be heated simultaneously.</p><p>The global concentric ring water bath market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
