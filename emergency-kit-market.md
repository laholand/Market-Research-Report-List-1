<p><p>An emergency kit, also known as a disaster supply kit or a survival kit, is a collection of essential items and supplies needed to sustain oneself during an emergency situation. Emergency kits are crucial in preparing for natural disasters, emergencies, or unexpected events that may lead to disruptions in basic services and infrastructure.</p><p>The global emergency kit market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. The pandemic has highlighted the need for individuals and organizations to have the necessary supplies to manage health emergencies. As a result, there has been an increased emphasis on personal protective equipment (PPE) in emergency kits, including masks, sanitizers, and gloves.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing trends such as the integration of advanced technologies in emergency kits.
