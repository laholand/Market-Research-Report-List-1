<p><p>Sweet whey powder is a popular dairy ingredient used in various food products. There are two types of sweet whey powder available in the market: fully demineralized and incomplete demineralized. Fully demineralized sweet whey powder undergoes a process that removes all minerals, resulting in a fine powder with a neutral taste. On the other hand, incomplete demineralized sweet whey powder retains some minerals, providing a slightly more distinct flavor. Both types offer different options for manufacturers based on their desired taste and nutritional requirements.</p></p>
