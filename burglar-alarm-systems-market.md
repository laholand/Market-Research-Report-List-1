<p><p>Burglar alarm systems can be categorized into two types: wireless alarm systems and wired alarm systems. Wireless alarm systems use radio frequency technology to communicate between the various components of the system, such as sensors and control panels. This allows for easy installation without the need for extensive wiring. On the other hand, wired alarm systems rely on physical wiring connections between the components. While they may require more installation effort, wired systems tend to be more reliable and less susceptible to hacking or signal interference.</p></p>
