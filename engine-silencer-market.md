<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The engine silencer market consists of three main types: resistive muffler, reactive muffler, and impedance muffler. A resistive muffler is designed to reduce noise by using insulation and absorption materials to dampen sound waves. Reactive mufflers use chambers and tubes to reflect sound waves and cancel out noise. Impedance mufflers work by using perforated tubes to create specific sound wave interference patterns that minimize noise. These types of mufflers are commonly used in vehicles and industrial machinery to reduce engine noise and comply with noise regulations.</p></p>
