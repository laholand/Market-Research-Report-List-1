Ball valves are a popular type of double block and bleed valves, offering tight shut-off functionality. Needle valves are another type, with a finely threaded stem for precise flow control. Both types are crucial in applications where safety and reliability are paramount, such as oil & gas, chemical, and petrochemical industries. They offer double isolation to prevent fluid leakage and a bleed to release any trapped pressure between the valves.</p></p>
