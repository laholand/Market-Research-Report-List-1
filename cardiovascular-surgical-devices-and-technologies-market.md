<p><p>Cardiovascular surgical devices and technologies refer to medical tools and equipment used in the treatment of heart and blood vessel diseases. Beating heart surgery systems involve performing surgical procedures on a still-beating heart. Perfusion disposables are single-use devices used in heart-lung machines to oxygenate and circulate blood during surgery. CPB equipment refers to cardiopulmonary bypass machines used to temporarily replace the pumping function of the heart during surgery. Cardiac ablation devices are used to treat abnormal heart rhythms by ablating or destroying the abnormal tissue causing the issue.</p></p>
