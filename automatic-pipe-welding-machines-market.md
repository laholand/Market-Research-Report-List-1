<p><p>Automatic pipe welding machines are utilized in various industries for efficient and precise welding processes. The market can be classified into three types based on the number of welding heads: dual head, single head, and others. Dual head machines consist of two welding heads that simultaneously perform welding on different sections of the pipe. Single head machines have a single welding head that welds the entire pipe. The "others" category includes machines with more than two welding heads or unique features, catering to specific welding requirements.</p></p>
