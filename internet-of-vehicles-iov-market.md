<p><p>The Internet of Vehicles (IoV) refers to the connectivity between vehicles and the internet, enabling communication and data exchange. Several market types exist within IoV, including Bluetooth, Cellular, Wi-Fi, NFC, and Others. Bluetooth technology enables short-range communication between devices, while Cellular networks allow vehicles to connect to the internet using cellular data networks. Wi-Fi enables vehicles to connect to the internet through wireless local area networks. NFC (Near Field Communication) technology facilitates short-range communication between vehicles and other devices. The "Others" category encompasses various emerging technologies used for internet connectivity in vehicles.</p></p>
