There are two main types of technologies used in these scanners: X-ray scanner and millimeter wave scanner. X-ray scanners produce images of the body using low-dose X-ray radiation and highlight any potentially dangerous items. On the other hand, millimeter wave scanners use non-ionizing electromagnetic waves to create a 3D image and identify hidden objects. Both technologies serve the purpose of enhancing airport security by providing thorough and efficient screening of individuals.</p></p>
