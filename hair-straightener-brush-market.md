<p><p>The market for hair straightener brushes can be categorized into two types: ionic hair straightener brushes and non-ionic hair straightener brushes. Ionic hair straightener brushes use negative ions to neutralize positive charges in the hair, reducing frizz and creating a sleek look. Non-ionic hair straightener brushes, on the other hand, do not use ion technology and primarily rely on the heat from the brush to straighten the hair. Both types of brushes are popular choices for individuals looking to achieve straight and sleek hair.</p></p>
