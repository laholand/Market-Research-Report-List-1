<p><strong>The Electric Heat Tracing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Regulating Wattage</li><li>Constant Wattage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric heat tracing systems are used to provide heat to pipes, vessels, and other equipment in order to prevent freezing or maintain temperature. There are two main types in the market: self-regulating wattage and constant wattage. Self-regulating wattage systems automatically adjust their output based on the temperature, increasing heat as needed and decreasing heat when not required. Constant wattage systems provide a consistent amount of heat output, regardless of temperature. Both systems have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
