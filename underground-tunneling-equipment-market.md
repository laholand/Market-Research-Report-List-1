<p><p>The underground tunneling equipment market consists of various types of machinery utilized for excavation purposes. Roadheaders are machines equipped with cutting heads that are used to create tunnels by grinding, drilling, or excavating the rock. They are commonly employed in smaller tunnels or for quick excavation projects. Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs), on the other hand, are massive machines used for longer and larger tunnel projects. TBMs operate by mechanically excavating the rocks and soil while simultaneously installing the tunnel lining, reducing the need for additional work.</p></p>
