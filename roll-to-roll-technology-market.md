<p><p>Roll-to-roll (R2R) technology market types consist of various printing methods used for mass production on flexible materials. Inkjet printing is a non-contact method that sprays tiny droplets of ink onto the substrate. Screen printing employs a mesh stencil to transfer ink onto the surface. Flexographic printing utilizes flexible relief plates to transfer ink from an anilox roller to the material. Gravure printing utilizes engraved cylinders to produce high-quality images. Other R2R technologies include offset lithography, letterpress printing, and hot foil stamping, each with its own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
