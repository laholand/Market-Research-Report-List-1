<p><p>A non-inflatable life jacket, also known as a foam life jacket, is a type of personal flotation device that does not require inflation to provide buoyancy. These life jackets are typically more durable and require little maintenance compared to their inflatable counterparts.</p><p>The non-inflatable life jacket market is steadily growing, and its future outlook looks promising.
