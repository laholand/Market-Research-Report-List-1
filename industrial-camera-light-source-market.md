<p><p>The industrial camera light source market comprises various types of light sources. Ring light sources are circular lights placed around the camera lens and provide uniform illumination. Bar light sources are long and thin lights used for line scan applications. Backlights are used to create a high contrast background for inspection purposes. Coaxial light sources emit light parallel to the optical axis, allowing for accurate surface inspection. Other types of light sources include dome lights and structured lights, each serving different industrial imaging purposes.</p></p>
