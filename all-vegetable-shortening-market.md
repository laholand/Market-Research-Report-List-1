<p><p>Non-Emulsion Type market refers to vegetable shortening that is solid at room temperature and typically made solely from vegetable oil. It does not contain any water or milk solids. This type of shortening is used in baked goods like pastries and cookies. On the other hand, Emulsion Type market refers to vegetable shortening that is made by blending vegetable oil with water. It is usually in a semi-solid or liquid form and is used in various food applications, such as frying, baking, and as a spread.</p></p>
