<p><p>The aircraft automatic pilot market includes three main types: single-axis, dual-axis, and 3-axis systems. Single-axis autopilots are the most basic, providing control of only one aircraft axis, usually the roll axis. Dual-axis autopilots, on the other hand, control two axes, typically roll and pitch, offering slightly more advanced functionality. Finally, 3-axis autopilots are the most sophisticated, controlling all three axes including roll, pitch, and yaw. These market types represent varying levels of automation and functionality in aircraft autopilot systems.</p></p>
