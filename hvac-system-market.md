<p><p>The HVAC system market consists of various types of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning equipment. Heat pumps are devices that transfer heat from one place to another and are commonly used for cooling and heating purposes. Furnaces are units that generate heat by burning fuel, such as natural gas or oil, and are primarily used for heating. Boilers utilize hot water or steam to provide heat and are commonly used in residential and commercial settings. Unitary heaters are self-contained systems that provide heating and cooling in a single unit.</p></p>
