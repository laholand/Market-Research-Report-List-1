<p><p>Metal release agents are used in various industries to prevent adhesion of molten metal to molds or tools. There are two main types of Metal Release Agent Market: Permanent and Semi-permanent. Permanent release agents create a durable barrier between the mold and the metal, providing long-lasting protection. Semi-permanent release agents offer a balance between durability and ease of mold release, requiring reapplication after a certain number of uses. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient metal casting processes.</p></p>
