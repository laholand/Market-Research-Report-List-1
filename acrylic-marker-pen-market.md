<p><p>The acrylic marker pen market offers three types of tips: broad tip, fine tip, and brush tip. Broad tip markers have a thick, chisel-shaped point that allows for wider strokes and coverage, making them suitable for larger designs or filling in spaces quickly. Fine tip markers have a thin point, making them ideal for detailed work or writing. Brush tip markers have a flexible, brush-like tip that allows for both thin and thick lines, making them versatile for a range of artistic techniques and styles.</p></p>
