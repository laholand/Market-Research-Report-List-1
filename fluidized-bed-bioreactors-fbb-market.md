<p><p>Fluidized-Bed Bioreactors (FBB) are specialized systems used for biological processes. Diversion-Type Microcapsule-Suspension Fluidized Bed Bioreactor (DMFBB) diverts microcapsules for efficient enzyme recovery. Circulating Fluidized-Bed Bioreactor (CFBBR) maintains biomass suspension through circulating motion. Laboratory-Scale Fluidized Bed Bioreactor (sFBB) is a smaller version for research purposes. Bio-Artificial Liver (BAL) market focuses on developing bioreactors that mimic the liver's function to provide support for patients with liver failure. These systems have unique applications in the biotechnology field and are continuously evolving to improve bioprocessing efficiency and medical treatments.</p></p>
