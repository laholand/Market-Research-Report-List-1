<p><p>The Electro-optic Pockels Cells market can be categorized into two types: Single Crystal and Double Crystal. Single Crystal Pockels Cells are made from a single crystal material and offer high efficiency and fast response time. They are commonly used in applications requiring high voltage and high power, such as laser amplifiers. On the other hand, Double Crystal Pockels Cells consist of two crystals that sandwich the electro-optic material. They provide better stability and lower optical damage, making them suitable for applications like Q-switching and mode-locking lasers.</p></p>
