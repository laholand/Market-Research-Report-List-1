<p><p>Pharmaceutical marijuana (MMJ) comes in different market types based on the levels of two main compounds: THC and CBD. THC-dominant products contain higher levels of tetrahydrocannabinol, which is responsible for the psychoactive effects and may help with pain relief, appetite stimulation, and nausea reduction. CBD-dominant products have higher cannabidiol levels, which provide therapeutic effects such as anti-inflammatory, anti-anxiety, and anti-seizure properties, without the psychoactive effects. Balanced THC and CBD products maintain a more equal ratio of these compounds, offering a combination of both psychoactive and therapeutic effects for various medical purposes.</p></p>
