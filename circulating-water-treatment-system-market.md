<p><p>The circulating water treatment system market consists of three main types: circulating water pumps, cooling towers, and CW treatment systems. Circulating water pumps are responsible for circulating water within a system to maintain temperature levels. Cooling towers are used to transfer heat from the circulating water by means of evaporation, keeping the system cool. CW treatment systems help in maintaining water quality by removing impurities and controlling microbial growth. All three components play a crucial role in the efficient operation of circulating water systems in various industries.</p></p>
