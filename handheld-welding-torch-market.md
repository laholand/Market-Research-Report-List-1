<p><p>The handheld welding torch market consists of different types, including MIG/MAG welding torches and TIG welding torches. MIG/MAG welding torches are commonly used for metal inert gas (MIG) or metal active gas (MAG) welding processes. They are suitable for a variety of welding applications and offer high efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, TIG welding torches are used for tungsten inert gas (TIG) welding, which provides precise and high-quality welds. TIG welding torches are commonly used in industries where the appearance and integrity of the welds are essential.</p></p>
