<p><p>The Compact Camera Module (CCM) market offers various types of modules to suit different technological requirements. One type is Chip-On-Board (COB) and Chip-On-Flex (COF), where the image sensor is directly mounted on the circuit board or flex substrate to ensure compactness. Another type is the Flip-Chip (FC) module, which uses flip-chip technology to bond the image sensor to the substrate. Additionally, there are other types in the market that provide different designs and functionalities to cater to diverse consumer demands.</p></p>
