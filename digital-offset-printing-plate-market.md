<p><ul><li>Thermal CTP Plate</li><li>UV-CTP Plates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital offset printing plate market is categorized into two main types: Thermal CTP Plates and UV-CTP Plates. Thermal CTP plates use heat to create the image on the plate, while UV-CTP plates use ultraviolet light. Both types offer high-quality printing results and improved efficiency compared to traditional offset printing plates. Thermal CTP plates are more commonly used due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of use, while UV-CTP plates are preferred for their environmental benefits and longer durability.</p></p>
