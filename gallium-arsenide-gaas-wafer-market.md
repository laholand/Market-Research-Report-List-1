<p><strong>The Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) wafer market can be divided into two main types - LEC Grown GaAs and VGF Grown GaAs. LEC Grown GaAs wafers are produced using Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski method, which results in high purity and high crystalline quality wafers. On the other hand, VGF Grown GaAs wafers are manufactured through the Vertical Gradient Freeze method, offering better control over material properties and higher carrier mobility. Both types have their own advantages and applications in industries such as telecommunications, aerospace, and solar energy.</p></p>
