<p><p>The portable audio codecs market includes two main types of technologies: analog and digital codecs. Analog codecs convert audio signals into analog format for transmission or storage. These codecs are typically used in older devices and have limitations in terms of sound quality and flexibility. On the other hand, digital codecs convert audio signals into digital format, resulting in higher sound quality and more efficient compression. Digital codecs are commonly found in modern portable audio devices, offering superior performance and compatibility with various formats.</p></p>
