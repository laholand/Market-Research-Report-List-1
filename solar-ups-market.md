<p><p>The Solar UPS market can be divided into two types based on power capacity: High Power Solar UPS and Low Power Solar UPS. High Power Solar UPS refers to solar-powered systems with a higher capacity, typically used for commercial and industrial applications. They provide backup power for large loads and require more solar panels for generating electricity. On the other hand, Low Power Solar UPS have lower power capacity and are commonly used for residential or small-scale applications. They provide backup power for smaller loads and require fewer solar panels for electricity generation. Both types utilize solar energy as a sustainable source for uninterrupted power supply.</p></p>
