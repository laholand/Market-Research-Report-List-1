<p><p>A forklift tipping skip is a type of attachment specifically designed for forklifts to transport and unload bulk materials. A tipping skip typically consists of a heavy-duty container with a hinged base that can be manually or hydraulically tipped to discharge its content.</p><p>The market for forklift tipping skips has been experiencing steady growth due to the increasing demand for efficient and safe material handling solutions. The primary drivers for this market growth include the rising construction activities, growing focus on waste management, and increasing usage of forklifts in agriculture.</p><p>In terms of market trends, manufacturers are focusing on developing lightweight tipping skips made from durable materials such as steel or high-density polyethylene (HDPE).
