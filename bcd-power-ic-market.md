<p><p>BCD Power IC (Bipolar-CMOS-DMOS) refers to a type of integrated circuit that combines bipolar, CMOS, and DMOS technologies to enable the integration of analog, digital, and power functions on a single chip. There are two main types of BCD Power ICs - High-Voltage BCD and High-Density BCD. </p><p>High-Voltage BCD technology allows for the integration of high-voltage (up to several hundred volts) power devices, making it suitable for applications like motor drivers, LED lighting, and power management systems. </p><p>On the other hand, High-Density BCD technology focuses on integrating a large number of devices, enabling advanced functions like digital signal processing and wireless communication in applications such as smartphones, tablets, and automotive systems.</p></p>
