<p><p>A nuts hulling machine is a specialized equipment used for removing the outer shell (hull) of various nuts such as almonds, cashews, walnuts, and hazelnuts. The machine works by cracking open the nutshell and then separating the hull from the kernel. The nuts are fed into the machine, where they go through a series of processes, including cracking, hulling, and separating.</p><p>The market for nuts hulling machines has been witnessing steady growth in recent years. Increasing demand for processed nuts, growing health consciousness among consumers, and the rise in disposable income are some of the key factors driving the market growth. Manufacturers are constantly focusing on innovations to improve the efficiency and productivity of these machines.
