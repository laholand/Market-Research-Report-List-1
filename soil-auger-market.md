<p><p>A soil auger is a tool used to collect soil samples from the ground for testing purposes. It consists of a long metal shaft with a helical-shaped blade at the bottom. Soil augers are commonly used in various industries such as agriculture, construction, geology, and environmental science.</p><p>The future outlook of the soil auger market appears to be promising.
