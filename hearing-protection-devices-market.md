<p><p>Hearing Protection Devices refer to devices designed to prevent or reduce damage to the ears caused by exposure to loud noises. Earplugs and earmuffs are two common types of these devices. Earplugs are small, typically made of foam or silicone, and are inserted into the ear canal to block sound. On the other hand, earmuffs are larger and cover the entire ear, creating a seal to block noise. Both types of devices offer different levels of protection and are widely used in various industries, construction sites, and recreational activities.</p></p>
