<p><p>The online accounting system market comprises three main types: </p><p>1) Browser-based: These systems are accessed through a web browser and do not require any software installation. Users can access their accounting data from any device with an internet connection.</p><p>2) Software as a Service (SaaS): These systems are cloud-based and provide access to accounting tools through a subscription model. They offer scalability, flexibility, and automatic updates, making them suitable for small to medium-sized businesses.</p><p>3) Application Service Providers (ASPs): These systems are similar to SaaS but operated by third-party vendors who host and manage the software on their own servers. Users access the application remotely, typically through the internet, and pay a fee for using the services.</p></p>
