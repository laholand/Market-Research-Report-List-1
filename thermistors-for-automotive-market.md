<p><p>Thermistors are temperature-sensitive devices used in the automotive industry. There are two types of thermistors in the market: PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient). PTC thermistors have a positive relationship between temperature and resistance. They are primarily used in automotive applications that require over-temperature protection, such as electric motors. On the other hand, NTC thermistors have a negative relationship between temperature and resistance. They are commonly used in automotive applications for temperature sensing, control, and monitoring, such as engine coolant temperature sensors.</p></p>
