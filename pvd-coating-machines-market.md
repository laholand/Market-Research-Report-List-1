<p><strong>The PVD Coating Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation Category</li><li>Sputtering Category</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVD coating machines are divided into two market types: Evaporation Category and Sputtering Category. In the Evaporation Category, PVD coatings are produced by evaporating the coating material in a vacuum chamber through the application of heat. This process allows for the creation of uniform, thin film coatings. In contrast, the Sputtering Category involves using high-energy ions to bombard the coating material, causing atoms to dislodge and deposit onto the substrate. This technique provides enhanced coating flexibility and is ideal for complex shapes and materials.</p></p>
