<p><p>The High Pressure Contrast Media Injector Market consists of three types: Single Head, Dual Head, and Syringeless injectors. Single Head injectors incorporate one injector head and are typically used in simple imaging procedures. Dual Head injectors feature two injector heads, enabling simultaneous injection of contrast media to enhance imaging efficiency. Syringeless injectors eliminate the need for disposable syringes, reducing cost and waste. These three market types cater to different imaging requirements, offering various functionalities and benefits for healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
