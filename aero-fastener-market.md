<p><p>The Aero Fastener market consists of two main types: Threaded Fasteners and Non-Threaded Fasteners. Threaded Fasteners, as the name suggests, have threads on their shafts that allow them to be screwed into place. These are commonly used to join two or more components together securely. On the other hand, Non-Threaded Fasteners do not have threads and are typically used for applications where the fastening needs to be quick and easy, such as with rivets or clips. Both types play a vital role in securing components and ensuring structural integrity in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
