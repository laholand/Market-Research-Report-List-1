<p><p>Eyeglass lens cleaner is a solution used to clean eyeglass lenses, removing dirt, smudges, and fingerprints. Eyeglass lens cleaners can be found in both liquid and spray form and are widely available in optical stores, online retailers, and even some grocery stores.</p><p>The eyeglass lens cleaner market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue on this upward trajectory. Consumers are becoming more conscious of the environmental impact of the products they use and are actively seeking lens cleaners that are free from harsh chemicals and are biodegradable.</p><p>Another trend is the introduction of lens cleaning wipes, which offer convenience and ease of use.
