<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Radiation Hardened Electronics Market consists of two main types: Radiation Hardening by Design (RHBD) and Radiation Hardening by Process (RHBP). RHBD involves designing electronic components with specific features to withstand radiation effects. This may include using radiation-resistant materials and implementing redundant circuits for fault tolerance. On the other hand, RHBP focuses on modifying the manufacturing process to enhance radiation resistance without altering the component's design. Both approaches aim to develop electronics that can operate reliably in high-radiation environments, such as in aerospace, defense, and nuclear power applications.</p></p>
