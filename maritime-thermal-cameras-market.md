<p><strong>The Maritime Thermal Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maritime thermal cameras are used in the maritime industry for various applications like navigation, surveillance, and search and rescue operations. There are two main types of thermal cameras available in the market: uncooled type and cooled type. </p><p>Uncooled type thermal cameras use microbolometers to detect infrared radiation and convert it into a visible image without the need for cooling mechanisms. They are cost-effective, smaller in size, and require less power. However, they have lower resolution and sensitivity compared to cooled type cameras.</p><p>Cooled type thermal cameras use a cryogenic cooling system to cool the infrared detector, enhancing their performance. They offer higher resolution, sensitivity, and longer detection ranges, making them suitable for long-range surveillance or marine security applications. However, they are bulkier, more expensive, and require continuous cooling.</p><p>Both types of cameras play a significant role in enhancing maritime situational awareness and ensuring safety at sea.</p></p>
