<p><p>The Neurostimulator Device Market includes two main types: Stationary Neurostimulators and Portable Neurostimulators. Stationary Neurostimulator devices are designed to be used in a fixed location, typically in a healthcare facility or clinical setting. They provide continuous or programmed electrical stimulation to specific areas of the body to alleviate chronic pain or manage certain medical conditions. On the other hand, Portable Neurostimulators are smaller and more lightweight devices that are intended for personal use. They allow individuals to conveniently and independently manage their pain or medical condition from any location.</p></p>
