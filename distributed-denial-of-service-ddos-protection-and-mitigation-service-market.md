<p><p>Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) Protection and Mitigation Service is a type of cybersecurity service that helps protect websites, networks, or online services from DDoS attacks. These attacks occur when multiple compromised systems, often referred to as a botnet, flood the targeted website or service with excessive traffic, causing it to become overwhelmed and inaccessible to legitimate users.</p><p>The main objective of DDoS protection and mitigation service is to ensure uninterrupted availability of online services by filtering out malicious traffic and allowing only legitimate requests to reach the target system. It typically involves a combination of hardware and software solutions, including firewalls, load balancers, and traffic analyzers, to detect and mitigate DDoS attacks in real-time.</p><p>The market for DDoS protection and mitigation services has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. This growth can be attributed to the increasing frequency and complexity of DDoS attacks, as well as the growing awareness among organizations about the potential impact of these attacks on their business operations. Additionally, the rising adoption of cloud-based services and the proliferation of connected devices, such as smartphones and IoT devices, have further fueled the demand for DDoS protection and mitigation services.</p><p>Moreover, the market is also witnessing the emergence of new trends.
