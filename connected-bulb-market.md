<p><p>The connected bulb market is primarily divided into three types: ZigBee, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth. ZigBee technology enables bulbs to connect wirelessly in a mesh network, making them ideal for larger scale applications. Wi-Fi bulbs connect directly to a network, allowing easy integration with other devices and remote access. Bluetooth bulbs connect to smartphones or tablets, providing a simple and convenient solution for controlling lighting in various settings. These different market types allow consumers to choose the technology that best suits their connectivity needs and desired functionality.</p></p>
