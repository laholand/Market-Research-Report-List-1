<p><p>Enterprise Feedback Management (EFM) software refers to a system designed to collect, analyze, and manage customer feedback and sentiments for businesses. The market for EFM software can be classified into two types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises EFM software is installed on the company's own servers, offering greater control over data and infrastructure, but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts. Conversely, Cloud-Based EFM software is hosted on a remote server and accessed through the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, while relying on third-party servers for data security and system reliability. Both types cater to varying business needs and preferences.</p></p>
