<p><p>Electric smart meters are devices used to measure and analyze electricity consumption in homes and businesses. There are two main types of smart meters: single-phase smart meters and three-phase smart meters. Single-phase smart meters are typically used in residential settings and are designed to measure electricity usage in single-phase electrical systems. Three-phase smart meters, on the other hand, are used in larger commercial or industrial settings with three-phase electrical systems. These meters provide more comprehensive data on electricity consumption, allowing for more accurate billing and better energy management.</p></p>
