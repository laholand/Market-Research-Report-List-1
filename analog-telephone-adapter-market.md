<p><p>Analog Telephone Adapter (ATA) is a device that converts analog telephone signals into digital signals for transmission over the internet. Single port ATA refers to a device that allows only one telephone to be connected and used for VoIP calls. On the other hand, multiport ATA enables multiple telephones to be connected and used simultaneously for VoIP calls, typically offering two, four, or more ports. Single port ATAs are suitable for home users or small businesses with few phones, while multiport ATAs are designed for larger organizations or call centers that require multiple phone connections.</p></p>
