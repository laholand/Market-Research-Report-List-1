<p><p>The Diagnostic Imaging Services Market comprises different types of imaging modalities used for medical diagnosis. Diagnostic Radiology employs X-rays to generate images of the body's internal structures. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of organs and tissues. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-rays and computer technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of the body's organs. Nuclear Imaging employs radioactive substances to visualize internal body functions. "Others" category may include emerging imaging techniques such as optical coherence tomography or positron emission tomography, among others.</p></p>
