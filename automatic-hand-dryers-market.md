<p><p>The automatic hand dryer market consists of two main types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use high-speed, focused air to quickly and efficiently dry hands. They usually have a sleek and compact design, making them suitable for small spaces. On the other hand, hot air dryers rely on warm air to dry hands. They are often larger in size and provide a more gentle drying experience. Both types aim to replace paper towels, offering a more sustainable and hygienic solution for hand drying in public restrooms.</p></p>
