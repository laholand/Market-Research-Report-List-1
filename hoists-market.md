Manual hoists require physical effort to operate and are suitable for light lifting applications. Electric hoists are powered by electricity and offer greater lifting capacity and control. Air hoists are pneumatic and use compressed air to lift heavy loads. Hydraulic hoists utilize hydraulic power to lift and lower objects, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
