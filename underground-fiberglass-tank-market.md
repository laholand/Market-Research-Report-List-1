<p><p>The underground fiberglass tank market is divided into two types: single wall and double wall tanks. Single wall underground fiberglass tanks are constructed using a single layer of fiberglass and are suitable for storing a variety of liquids such as water, oil, or chemicals. On the other hand, double wall underground fiberglass tanks consist of two layers of fiberglass with an interstitial space between them, providing an additional safety measure against leaks or spills. Both types of tanks are commonly used in industries where underground storage is required.</p></p>
