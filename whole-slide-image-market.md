<p><p>The Whole Slide Image market consists of two main types: Brightfield and Fluorescence. Brightfield imaging involves using standard light microscopy to capture images that show tissue samples in their natural state. This type of imaging is commonly used for pathology and routine analysis. On the other hand, Fluorescence imaging utilizes fluorescent dyes and specialized microscopes to examine specific molecules within cells or tissues. This type of imaging is valuable for studying cellular structures and protein localization, often used in research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
