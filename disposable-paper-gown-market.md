<p><p>The disposable paper gown market can be classified into two types: paper-based and plastic-based. The paper-based gowns are made from cellulose fibers obtained from trees and are biodegradable, making them more environmentally friendly. On the other hand, plastic-based gowns are made from synthetic materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, which offer a higher level of protection against liquids and chemicals. While both types serve the purpose of providing single-use protective clothing, their composition differs, leading to variations in durability, comfort, and environmental impact.</p></p>
