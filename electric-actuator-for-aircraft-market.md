<p><ul><li>Linear Motion</li><li>Rotary Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric actuator for aircraft market includes two main types: linear motion and rotary motion. Linear motion actuators enable back-and-forth movement in a straight line, commonly used for functions like opening/closing doors or extending/retracting landing gear. On the other hand, rotary motion actuators allow for rotational movement, such as controlling aircraft engine valves or adjusting wing flaps. Both types are essential for various operations in aircraft, with linear motion actuators providing linear movement and rotary motion actuators enabling rotational control.</p></p>
