<p><p>Gear box refers to a device that is used to transmit mechanical power from one component to another in a machine. It is commonly used to increase torque while reducing the speed of the output shaft. Gear motors, on the other hand, combine an electric motor with a gear box to deliver high torque output. They are widely used in various industries and applications such as automotive, machinery, aerospace, robotics, and many more.</p><p>The gear box and gear motors market has been experiencing steady growth over the years.
