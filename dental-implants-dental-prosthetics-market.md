<p><p>Dental implants and dental prosthetics market can be categorized into four main types based on the materials used: metals, polymers, ceramics, and biomaterials. Metal implants are often made of titanium and provide strong support for replacement teeth. Polymers are lightweight and flexible, making them suitable for temporary prosthetics. Ceramics are durable and aesthetically pleasing, mimicking the natural appearance of teeth. Biomaterials are biocompatible materials that promote tissue integration, improving the success rate of implants and prosthetics.</p></p>
