<p><strong>The VR Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Fresnel Lens</li><li>Fresnel Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VR Lens market consists of two main types: Non-Fresnel Lens and Fresnel Lens. Non-Fresnel Lens is a traditional lens design that uses a curved surface to bend light, resulting in clear and distortion-free visuals. It provides accurate focus and minimal aberration. On the other hand, Fresnel Lens is a thinner and lighter lens that uses concentric ridges to focus light, making it suitable for compact VR devices. Although it may introduce slight artifacts and reduced clarity, Fresnel Lens offers a wider field of view and improved overall VR immersion.</p></p>
