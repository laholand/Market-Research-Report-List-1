<p><p>Self-injection systems refer to devices that allow individuals to administer medication by themselves. There are two main types of self-injection systems: fillable and pre-filled. Fillable systems enable patients to draw a specific dose of medication using a syringe and inject it into their body. Both types provide convenience and allow patients to self-administer medication easily and safely.</p></p>
