<p><p>Colorectal cancer market types include chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and immunotherapy. Chemotherapy involves the use of drugs that kill or slow down the growth of cancer cells throughout the body. Targeted therapy focuses on specific molecules or genes involved in cancer growth to inhibit their activity. Immunotherapy boosts the body's immune system to recognize and destroy cancer cells. These treatments aim to improve patient outcomes by reducing tumor size, preventing metastasis, and increasing survival rates. Each market type offers distinct advantages and may be used alone or in combination depending on the patient's condition.</p></p>
