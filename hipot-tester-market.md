AC Hipot Testers are used to measure the strength of insulation in electrical systems operating with alternating current. On the other hand, DC Hipot Testers evaluate the insulation integrity in systems that use direct current. Both types of testers are crucial in ensuring the safety and reliability of electrical equipment and are widely employed across industries such as manufacturing, electronics, and power utilities.</p></p>
