<p><p>Plasma treaters are devices used to modify the surface properties of various materials for better adhesion and bonding. Ground type plasma treaters are stationary machines that are typically installed in manufacturing plants and designed for large-scale production. They provide consistent and efficient treatment for high volume operations. On the other hand, portable plasma treaters are compact and lightweight units that can be easily transported and used on-site, making them ideal for smaller scale operations or when mobility is required. Both market types cater to different needs and applications within the plasma treater industry.</p></p>
