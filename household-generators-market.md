<p><p>The household generators market can be categorized into two types: portable and standby generators. Portable generators are compact and lightweight, designed for temporary use during power outages or outdoor activities. They are easy to transport and typically run on gasoline or propane. Standby generators, on the other hand, are installed permanently outside the home and connected directly to the electrical system. They automatically switch on during power outages and provide backup power until the main power source is restored. They are usually powered by natural gas or liquid propane.</p></p>
