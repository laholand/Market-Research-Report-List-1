<p><p>Plastic Bottles & Containers market is categorized into four main types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). PET bottles are commonly used for beverages and food packaging due to their lightweight and durable nature. PP containers are known for their resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for storing household and industrial products. HDPE bottles are popular for their strength and are commonly used for milk jugs and detergent bottles. LDPE containers are flexible and used for items like squeeze bottles and plastic bags.</p></p>
