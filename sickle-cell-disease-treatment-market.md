<p><p>Sickle Cell Disease Treatment Market includes various types of the disease such as Sickle Cell Anemia, Sickle Beta Thalassemia, and Sickle Hemoglobin C Disease. Sickle Cell Anemia is the most common and severe form, characterized by abnormal hemoglobin production leading to misshapen red blood cells. Sickle Beta Thalassemia is a combination of sickle cell traits and thalassemia, resulting in reduced production of normal hemoglobin. Sickle Hemoglobin C Disease occurs when individuals inherit both sickle cell traits and hemoglobin C gene, causing similar symptoms as sickle cell anemia. Different treatment options exist for managing each of these specific conditions.</p></p>
