<p><p>The medical coatings market comprises various types of coatings used in the healthcare industry. Hydrophilic coatings enhance the lubricity of medical devices, reducing friction during procedures. Antimicrobial coatings prevent the growth of bacteria and other microorganisms on medical devices, reducing the risk of infections. Antithrombotic coatings inhibit the formation of blood clots on implants and related medical equipment. Drug delivery coatings facilitate controlled and targeted release of drugs from medical devices. Other types of coatings include anti-fouling coatings, lubricious coatings, and anti-corrosion coatings, which offer additional functionalities to medical devices.</p></p>
