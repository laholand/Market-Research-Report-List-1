<p><p>The Seismic Support System market includes three types of support systems: Cylindrical Support, Bushing Support, and Conical Support. Cylindrical Support refers to cylindrical-shaped structures used to provide stability and resistance against seismic activity. Bushing Support involves the use of bushings, which are rubber or elastomeric devices, to absorb seismic forces and reduce the impact on the structure. Conical Support entails the use of conical-shaped devices to enhance the structural performance during seismic events. These systems are designed to protect buildings and infrastructure by absorbing and dissipating seismic energy.</p></p>
