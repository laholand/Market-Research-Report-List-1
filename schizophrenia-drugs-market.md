<p><p>The schizophrenia drugs market is divided into two types: oral antipsychotics and injectable antipsychotics. Oral antipsychotics are medications that come in the form of tablets or capsules and are taken by mouth. They are commonly prescribed for long-term treatment of schizophrenia. On the other hand, injectable antipsychotics are administered through injections and are often used when a patient is not able to take oral medications or requires rapid symptom control. Both types of medications aim to manage the symptoms of schizophrenia and improve patients' overall quality of life.</p></p>
