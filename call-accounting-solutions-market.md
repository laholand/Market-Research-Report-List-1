<p><p>Call accounting solutions can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based call accounting solutions refer to systems that are hosted and operated on remote servers accessed via the internet. These solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as there is no need for hardware installation or maintenance. On the other hand, on-premises call accounting solutions are installed and maintained locally on the company's own servers. Although they require upfront investment and dedicated IT support, they provide greater control, security, and customization options for organizations.</p></p>
