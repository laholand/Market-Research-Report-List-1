<p><p>Roof drains are designed to drain water off the roofs of buildings and prevent waterlogging. There are several types of roof drains available in the market. Sill type drains are installed on the edge of the roof and allow water to flow out. Parapet type drains are located on the top of a parapet wall, effectively draining water from the roof. Flow control type drains are equipped with a mechanism to regulate the water flow. Overflow type drains prevent flooding by redirecting excess water. Lastly, there are other types of roof drains available in the market that cater to specific requirements or designs.</p></p>
