<p><p>The Automatic Train Protection (ATP) signaling system market consists of three main types: ATP, ATO, and ATS. ATP ensures the train maintains a safe distance from other trains and stops at signals. ATO (Automatic Train Operation) automates train operations, including starting, stopping, and running at optimal speeds. ATS (Automatic Train Supervision) monitors train movements and coordinates their activities. All three systems use advanced technologies to enhance safety and efficiency in railway operations, with ATP being the foundation of these automated systems.</p></p>
