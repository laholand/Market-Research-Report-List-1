<p><p>Digital tachometers are devices used to measure rotational speed in various applications. There are typically two types of digital tachometers available in the market: AC and DC. AC tachometers are designed to measure the speed of alternating current systems, commonly found in motors and generators. On the other hand, DC tachometers are used to measure the rotational speed of direct current systems, often found in applications like automotive engines and industrial machinery. Both types of tachometers provide accurate speed measurements for their respective systems, helping users monitor and control the rotational speed as needed.</p></p>
