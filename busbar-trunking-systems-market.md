<p><p>Busbar trunking systems are electrical distribution systems used to safely transmit power in large buildings or industrial facilities. They consist of a series of prefabricated electrical busbars, which carry the electrical current to various points of use through tap-off units. This eliminates the need for individual cables and reduces the risk of faults or short circuits.</p><p>The current market outlook for busbar trunking systems is positive and is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
