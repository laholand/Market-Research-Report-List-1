<p><p>The subwoofers market consists of two main types: powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers have built-in amplifiers, which means they can operate independently without needing additional amplification equipment. They are generally more convenient and have more control options. On the other hand, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier to function and are typically less expensive. They are commonly used in professional audio setups where separate amplifiers are already available. Both powered and passive subwoofers cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
