<p><strong>The Marine Inverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outboards</li><li>Inboards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine inverter market can be categorized into two types: outboards and inboards. Outboard marine inverters are designed specifically for smaller boats and typically have a portable and detachable design, allowing for easy installation and flexibility. On the other hand, inboard marine inverters are more commonly used in larger boats and yachts. They are typically installed inside the boat and provide a more powerful and stable power supply. Both types of marine inverters play a crucial role in converting the DC power from batteries to AC power for various electrical components on boats.</p></p>
