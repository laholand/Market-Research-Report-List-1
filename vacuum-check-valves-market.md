<p><p>Vacuum check valves are devices used to ensure the flow of fluids in one direction and prevent backflow. Lift check valves operate by using a disc that opens and closes vertically. Swing check valves utilize a hinged disc that swings open and shut. Butterfly check valves involve a disc that rotates on a vertical or horizontal axis. These valves are chosen based on factors like flow requirements, pressure levels, and the type of fluid being handled, to ensure efficient and reliable operation in various industrial applications.</p></p>
