<p><p>Double expansion anchors are mechanical fasteners used to securely fix objects or structures to concrete, brick, or other solid materials. These anchors consist of two expanding wings connected by a threaded stud. When the anchor is inserted into a pre-drilled hole, tightening the nut or bolt expands the wings, providing a strong and secure hold.</p><p>The global double expansion anchors market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing construction activities across the globe, particularly in emerging economies, are driving the demand for double expansion anchors.
