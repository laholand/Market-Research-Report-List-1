In Situ Hybridization is used to visualize specific nucleic acid sequences in cells or tissues. Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology enables amplification of DNA at a constant temperature, providing a rapid and efficient method for molecular diagnostics in oncology.</p></p>
