<p><p>Ultrafine silica fume, also known as microsilica, is available in three main types in the market. Densified silica fume has been processed to remove impurities and increase bulk density for easier handling. Semi-densified silica fume is partially processed and has a lower bulk density compared to densified silica fume. Undensified silica fume is in its raw form and has the lowest bulk density. These different types cater to various applications in industries such as construction, oil & gas, and electronics.</p></p>
