<p><p>Plastic protective packaging market is categorized into various types such as PP, PVC, HDPE, LDPE, and others. High-density polyethylene (HDPE) packaging provides good moisture resistance and strength. Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) packaging is flexible and easy to handle. The "others" category includes various plastic packaging materials like PET, polystyrene, and polyurethane, each with their own unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
