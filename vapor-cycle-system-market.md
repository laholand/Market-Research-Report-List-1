<p><p>The Vapor Cycle System, also known as the Rankine cycle, is a thermodynamic process used to convert heat energy into mechanical work. It utilizes a closed-loop system in which a working fluid, typically water, is heated and vaporized, then expanded through a turbine to generate power before being condensed and returned to its initial state. The Vapor Cycle System finds extensive application in power plants, refrigeration systems, and air conditioning units.</p><p>The Vapor Cycle System market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors.
