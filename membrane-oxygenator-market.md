<p><strong>The Membrane Oxygenator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Sheet Membrane Oxygenator</li><li>Hollow Fibre Membrane Oxygenator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane oxygenators are medical devices used in cardiopulmonary bypass procedures to exchange oxygen and carbon dioxide in the blood. The Flat Sheet Membrane Oxygenator type consists of thin sheets with microchannels where blood and gas flow through, allowing efficient gas exchange. On the other hand, the Hollow Fibre Membrane Oxygenator type utilizes bundles of hollow fibers where blood and gas flow separately, allowing optimal gas transfer. Both types are widely used in cardiac surgeries and critical care settings to provide oxygenation and maintain proper blood gas levels.</p></p>
