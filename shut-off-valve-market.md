<p><p>The shut-off valve market encompasses different types of valves, including ball valves, butterfly valves, gate valves, and globe valves. Ball valves have a spherical disc that controls flow. Butterfly valves use a rotating disc to regulate flow. Gate valves have a sliding gate mechanism for opening and closing flow passages. Globe valves employ a plug-like disc that moves up and down to regulate flow. Each valve type has specific applications and advantages, catering to different industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
