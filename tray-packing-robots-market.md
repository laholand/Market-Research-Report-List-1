<p><p>Tray packing robots are categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic tray packing robots are fully automated systems that can independently handle the entire tray packing process, from loading products to sealing the tray. They require minimal human intervention and can significantly improve efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, semi-automatic tray packing robots require some level of human assistance. While they can perform certain tasks autonomously, such as product placement, they still rely on human operators for tasks like tray loading and sealing.</p></p>
