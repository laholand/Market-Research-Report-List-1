<p><ul><li>VHF</li><li>UHF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Portable Base Station (PBS) is a mobile communication device that provides temporary or backup coverage in areas without regular network access. PBS market types are categorized by the frequency band they operate in, namely VHF (Very High Frequency) and UHF (Ultra High Frequency). VHF-based PBS units utilize the VHF frequency range between 30 and 300 MHz, offering better signal propagation over long distances but less data capacity. Conversely, UHF-based PBS units operate in the frequency range between 300 and 3000 MHz, providing higher data capacity but shorter coverage distances due to limited signal propagation.</p></p>
