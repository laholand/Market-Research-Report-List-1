<p><p>The high-performance tire yarn market is divided into two major types: polyamide yarn and polyester yarn. Polyamide yarn is a type of synthetic fiber known for its high strength and excellent abrasion resistance, making it suitable for use in high-performance tire manufacturing. On the other hand, polyester yarn is also a synthetic fiber characterized by its high durability, low shrinkage, and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Both types of yarn play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and longevity of tires, ensuring better traction, stability, and safety on the road.</p></p>
