<p><strong>The Veterinary Thermography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li><li>Mid-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li><li>Long-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary thermography market can be divided into three types of cameras based on the wavelength they operate on. Short-wavelength thermography cameras use a wavelength below 3µm and are commonly used for surface temperature measurements. Mid-wavelength thermography cameras operate between 3µm and 5µm and are effective in detecting deeper tissue conditions. Long-wavelength thermography cameras have a wavelength above 8µm, enabling them to capture heat emissions from internal organs and tissues. These different types cater to specific veterinary applications and contribute to the overall growth of the market.</p></p>
