<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automatic Deformation Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geodetic Measuring Devices</li><li>Geotechnical Measuring Devices</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Deformation Monitoring System (ADMS) market can be divided into three types. Firstly, the Geodetic Measuring Devices include instruments such as GPS receivers and total stations that measure deformations in structures and landforms accurately using geodetic principles. Secondly, the Geotechnical Measuring Devices use sensors like inclinometers and tiltmeters to monitor deformations in soil and rocks, providing crucial information for construction and stability analysis. Lastly, the Others category encompasses specialized ADMS devices tailored for specific applications like underground mining or landslide monitoring. These types cater to different monitoring needs and contribute to the overall ADMS market.</p></p>
