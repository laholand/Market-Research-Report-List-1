<p><p>Chemotherapy devices refer to medical equipment used for administering chemotherapy drugs to patients. There are different types of chemotherapy devices available in the market. </p><p>- LVP (Large Volume Pump) is a device that delivers chemotherapy drugs in larger volumes. It is typically used for patients requiring a higher dose or longer infusion duration.</p><p>- Syringe pump is a device that delivers chemotherapy drugs in smaller volumes, providing precise control over the infusion rate. It is commonly used for patients requiring lower doses or shorter infusion durations.</p><p>- Elastomeric pumps are portable devices that rely on the elasticity of a balloon-like reservoir to deliver chemotherapy drugs. They are typically used for ambulatory patients who need continuous medication administration.</p></p>
