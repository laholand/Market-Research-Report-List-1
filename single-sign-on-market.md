<p><p>Single sign-on (SSO) is a method that allows users to access multiple applications or systems with just one set of login credentials. SSO solutions are categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based SSO is hosted and managed by a third-party service provider, offering scalability, accessibility, and lower infrastructure costs. On-premise SSO, on the other hand, is deployed and managed directly by organizations within their own infrastructure, providing control, customization, and higher security but requiring more maintenance and resources. Each market type suits different organizational needs and preferences in providing a seamless and secure authentication experience.</p></p>
