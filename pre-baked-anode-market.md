<p><p>The Pre-Baked Anode market is categorized into three main types: CA-I, CA-II, and CA-III. CA-I represents pre-baked anodes used in high-amperage aluminum electrolysis cells, typically above 300 kA. CA-II refers to those used in electrolysis cells with medium amperage, usually between 200 kA and 300 kA. Lastly, CA-III denotes pre-baked anodes employed in low-amperage electrolysis cells, typically below 200 kA. These distinctions are essential as the anodes' composition and quality requirements differ according to the specific amperage capacity of the electrolysis cells they are designed for.</p></p>
