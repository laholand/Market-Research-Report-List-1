<p><ul><li>Side-Mount Floor Locks</li><li>Top-Mount Floor Locks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor locks are essential equipment used to secure various types of machinery and equipment in place. There are two primary types of floor locks available in the market: side-mount floor locks and top-mount floor locks. Side-mount floor locks are attached to the sides of equipment and provide stability by locking wheels in a fixed position. On the other hand, top-mount floor locks are installed on the top surface of equipment and secure them by pressing down on the floor. Both types serve the same purpose of preventing unwanted movement and ensuring safety during operation.</p></p>
