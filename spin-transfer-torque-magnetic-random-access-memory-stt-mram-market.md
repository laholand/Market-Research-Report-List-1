<p><p>Spin-transfer Torque Magnetic Random Access Memory (STT-MRAM) is a type of non-volatile memory that uses spin-transfer torque to switch the magnetization of a magnetic tunnel junction (MTJ). This technology has gained popularity in recent years due to its potential for high-speed, high-density, and low-power memory applications.</p><p>STT-MRAM offers several advantages over traditional memory technologies such as SRAM and DRAM. It provides faster read and write speeds, lower power consumption, and higher density, making it suitable for use in a wide range of applications, including smartphones, computers, automotive systems, and IoT devices.</p><p>The market for STT-MRAM is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
