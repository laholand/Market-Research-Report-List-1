<p><p>Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) battery is a type of valve-regulated lead-acid (VRLA) battery specifically designed to provide high performance and superior power in various applications. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat separator to hold the electrolyte solution in place, which is then absorbed by the matting, enabling the battery to deliver power efficiently. </p><p>The market for AGM batteries has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a promising rate. The increasing demand for AGM batteries can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, AGM batteries offer numerous advantages such as improved durability, minimal maintenance, and resistance to vibrations, making them ideal for use in automotive, industrial, and renewable energy applications. Additionally, the growing adoption of AGM batteries in electric vehicles and hybrid electric vehicles is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>One of the significant trends in the AGM battery market is the increasing focus on the development of advanced technologies and materials to enhance battery performance.
