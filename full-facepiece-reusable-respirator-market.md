<p><p>The full facepiece reusable respirator market can be categorized into three types based on their air supply sources. Self-contained air supply respirators have an integrated air source, typically a tank of compressed air, making them independent and portable. Gas cylinder supply respirators rely on external gas cylinders for their air source, allowing for longer usage periods. Dual air supply respirators offer the flexibility of both self-contained air supply and gas cylinder supply options, providing users with more versatility in different environments. These market types cater to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
