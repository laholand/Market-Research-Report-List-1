<p><p>Single Use Pumps are categorized into different market types based on the materials used for their construction. PP pumps, also known as polypropylene pumps, are made from a durable and chemically resistant plastic called polypropylene. These pumps are ideal for applications involving corrosive chemicals. PE pumps, made from polyethylene, are suitable for situations requiring a higher level of flexibility and resistance to impact. Other market types consist of pumps made from different materials such as PVC, PTFE, or PVDF, which are designed to meet specific needs in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and water treatment.</p></p>
