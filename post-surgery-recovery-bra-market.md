<p><strong>The Post Surgery Recovery Bra Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Opening Bra</li><li>Rear Opening Bra</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post-surgery recovery bras are available in two main types: front-opening bras and rear-opening bras. Front-opening bras have a closure at the front, allowing for easy access and removal without the need to strain or stretch the arms. These bras are beneficial for individuals with restricted arm movement after surgery. Rear-opening bras, on the other hand, have closures at the back, providing support and comfort while avoiding pressure on the surgical area. Both types cater to the specific needs of individuals during their recovery process after surgery.</p></p>
