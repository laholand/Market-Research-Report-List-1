<p><p>Rapid diagnostic testing for influenza, commonly known as RIDTs, includes two main market types: digital RIDTs and conventional RIDTs. Digital RIDTs utilize advanced technology to provide quick and accurate results for influenza diagnosis. These tests often involve the use of cartridges or cassettes with built-in sensors to detect viral antigens. On the other hand, conventional RIDTs are more traditional and rely on visual interpretations of test lines to determine the presence of the influenza virus. Both types help healthcare professionals diagnose influenza faster, allowing for timely treatment and prevention measures.</p></p>
