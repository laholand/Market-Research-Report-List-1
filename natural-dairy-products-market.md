Milk powder is dehydrated milk that undergoes a drying process to increase its shelf life. Cheese and butter are dairy products made from milk, with cheese being produced by coagulating milk proteins and butter derived from churning milk or cream. Lastly, the ice cream market includes frozen desserts made from milk or cream, often combined with flavors and other ingredients to create a creamy treat.</p></p>
