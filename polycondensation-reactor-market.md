<p><p>The polycondensation reactor market can be classified into two main types: horizontal polycondensation reactors and vertical polycondensation reactors. </p><p>Horizontal polycondensation reactors are designed with a horizontal cylindrical shape and are commonly used for low-viscosity reactions. They offer benefits like ease of maintenance and efficient heat transfer. </p><p>On the other hand, vertical polycondensation reactors have a vertical cylindrical shape and are typically used for high-viscosity reactions. They offer advantages such as better product quality control, uniform mixing, and higher production capacity.</p></p>
