<p><p>Automatic slack adjusters are devices used in the braking system of vehicles to automatically compensate for brake pad wear. There are two main types of slack adjusters: manual slack adjusters and automatic slack adjusters.</p><p>Manual slack adjusters require manual adjustment by the vehicle operator to compensate for brake pad wear. These adjusters are commonly found in older vehicles and require regular maintenance and inspection to ensure proper brake performance.</p><p>On the other hand, automatic slack adjusters, as the name suggests, automatically adjust for brake pad wear. They are self-adjusting and do not require manual intervention. These adjusters are commonly used in newer vehicles and provide improved safety and efficiency by ensuring consistent brake performance.</p></p>
