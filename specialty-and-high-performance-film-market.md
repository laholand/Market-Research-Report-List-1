Nylon films are commonly used in food packaging due to their barrier properties. Fluoropolymer films offer high chemical resistance and are often used in medical and industrial applications. Polycarbonate films are valued for their optical clarity and impact resistance, making them suitable for displays and automotive applications.</p></p>
