<p><strong>The Optical Handheld Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Readers</li><li>Linear Imager Readers</li><li>2D Imager Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical handheld readers refer to devices used for scanning and reading barcodes or other types of optically printed information. There are three main types of optical handheld readers available in the market. Laser readers use a laser beam to scan and read barcodes and are known for their long-range scanning capabilities. Linear imager readers capture an image of the barcode and use software algorithms to decode the information. 2D imager readers have the ability to read both 1D and 2D barcodes, providing more versatility and compatibility with various types of information.</p></p>
