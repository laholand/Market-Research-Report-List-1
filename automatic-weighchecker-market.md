<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic weighcheckers come in two main market types: In-Motion Checkweighers and Intermittent Checkweighers. In-Motion Checkweighers are used for high-speed production lines where products are weighed while in motion, ensuring accurate weight measurements without disrupting the flow of the line. Intermittent Checkweighers, on the other hand, are used for slower production lines where products are weighed periodically as they pass through the machine. Each market type caters to different production speeds and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
