<p><p>A Roots blower, also known as a positive displacement blower, is a type of rotary blower that is used to deliver a constant volume of air or gas at a high pressure. Roots blowers are widely used in various industries such as wastewater treatment, power generation, chemical processing, and pneumatic conveying.</p><p>The current outlook of the Roots blower market is moderately positive. The market has witnessed steady growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for efficient and reliable blowers in various industrial applications.
