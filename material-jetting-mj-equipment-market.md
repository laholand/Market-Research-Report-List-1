<p><p>Material Jetting (MJ) Equipment market includes three types: Ink Jetting, Binder Jetting, and Aerosol Jetting. Ink Jetting utilizes inkjet technology to deposit liquid droplets of material onto a substrate to form layers. Binder Jetting involves the application of a liquid binding agent onto powder materials, which are then selectively solidified into layers. Aerosol Jetting employs a high-precision nozzle to deposit an aerosolized material onto a substrate, allowing for the fabrication of intricate, high-resolution structures. These types of material jetting enable precise, complex 3D printing applications in various industries.</p></p>
