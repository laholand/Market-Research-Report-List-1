<p><p>Breast surgery retractors are used to hold and retract breast tissue during surgical procedures. There are two main types of retractors: single-arm retractors and double-arm retractors. </p><p>Single-arm retractors have a single arm that provides retraction on one side of the incision, allowing surgeons to access the surgical site. These retractors are suitable for smaller incisions or when retraction is required on only one side.</p><p>Double-arm retractors, on the other hand, have two arms that provide retraction on both sides of the incision. This type of retractor is suitable for larger incisions and when retraction is needed on both sides of the surgical site simultaneously. </p><p>Both single-arm and double-arm retractors play a crucial role in breast surgery by improving visibility and facilitating precise surgical maneuvers.</p></p>
