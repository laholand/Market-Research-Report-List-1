<p><p>The robo advisory market can be categorized into two types: pure robo advisory and hybrid robo advisory. Pure robo advisory refers to a completely automated platform that provides financial advice and investment management services without any human intervention. These platforms use algorithms and machine learning to offer customized investment portfolios based on the user's risk tolerance and financial goals. On the other hand, hybrid robo advisory combines automated features with access to human financial advisors. This model offers a personalized touch by allowing users to consult with professionals for additional guidance and support.</p></p>
