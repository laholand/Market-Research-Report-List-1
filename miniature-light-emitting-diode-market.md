<p><p>The Miniature Light Emitting Diode (LED) market can be categorized into three types: Backward Turned Stripes (BTS), Backward Turned Verticals (BTV), and Others. In the BTS market, LEDs are arranged in a pattern where the electrical connections are on the back side of the chip. BTV LEDs have vertically positioned electrical connections, allowing for higher brightness and efficiency. The Others market represents LED products that do not fall into the BTS or BTV categories. These LED types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, offering diverse options in the miniature LED market.</p></p>
