<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Contract catering service market types include fixed price contract and cost plus contract. In a fixed price contract, the catering service provider charges a fixed price for their services, regardless of the actual costs incurred. This type of contract provides predictable costs for the client. On the other hand, a cost plus contract involves the catering service provider billing the client for the actual costs incurred, plus an agreed-upon margin or fee. This type of contract allows for flexibility in pricing and can incentivize the supplier to manage costs efficiently.</p></p>
