In the pharmaceutical industry, salt is utilized in the production of medicines and intravenous solutions. In agriculture, it is used as a nutrient supplement for livestock and crops. In water treatment, refined salt is employed in desalination and water softening processes.</p><p>The latest trends in the refined salt market include the introduction of low-sodium and mineral-enriched variants. Increasing health consciousness among consumers has created a demand for salt with reduced sodium content.
