<p><strong>The Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam (XLPP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physically Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam</li><li>Chemically Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam (XLPP) is a lightweight material with excellent insulation properties. Physically Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam is created through a process of heating and foaming, resulting in a closed-cell structure that provides thermal insulation. Chemically Cross-linked Polypropylene Foam is made by using chemical agents to create cross-links between polymer chains, offering improved mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals. Both types of XLPP are widely used in packaging, automotive, construction, and other industries where insulation and cushioning are required.</p></p>
