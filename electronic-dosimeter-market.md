<p><strong>The Electronic Dosimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TLD Dosimeters</li><li>OSL Dosimeters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic dosimeters are devices used to measure and record cumulative radiation exposure. There are mainly three types of electronic dosimeters on the market. TLD (thermoluminescent dosimeters) dosimeters use special crystals that emit light when exposed to radiation. OSL (optically stimulated luminescence) dosimeters utilize light signals emitted from aluminum oxide crystals. Both TLD and OSL dosimeters require laboratory readings for data analysis. The "others" category includes advanced electronic dosimeters with features like real-time monitoring or alarm systems. These dosimeters provide accurate and reliable radiation dose measurements for radiation workers in various industries.</p></p>
