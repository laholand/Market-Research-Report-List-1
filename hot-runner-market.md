<p><p>Hot runner refers to a system used in plastic injection molding that eliminates the need for runners or sprues and efficiently delivers molten plastic into the mold cavities. This technology helps to improve productivity in the manufacturing process by reducing cycle times, material waste, and manual intervention. <p><strong>The Hot Runner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Gate Hot Runner</li><li>Valve Gate Hot Runner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot Runner market consists of various types: Open Gate Hot Runner and Valve Gate Hot Runner. Open Gate Hot Runner is a type where the gates remain open, allowing continuous flow of molten plastic during the injection molding process. This type is cost-effective and suitable for simple plastic parts. On the other hand, Valve Gate Hot Runner has gates that open and close during the injection process, enabling precise control over the flow of plastic. This type is preferred for complex and high-quality plastic products, as it minimizes defects and enhances part quality.</p></p>
