<p><p>Patient lifts refer to devices used to assist healthcare professionals in lifting and transferring patients with limited mobility. There are primarily two types of patient lifts in the market: manual lifts and power lifts. Manual lifts require physical force from the healthcare professional or caregiver to operate, making them suitable for light to moderate lifting. On the other hand, power lifts are motorized devices that require less physical exertion, making them ideal for heavier patients or frequent transfers. Each type of patient lift offers unique benefits and functionality, catering to different lifting needs in healthcare settings.</p></p>
