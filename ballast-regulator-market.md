<p><p>The ballast regulator market can be categorized into three types: fixed wing, rotating wing, and articulated rotating wing. In the fixed wing market, the ballast regulator has a stable structure and is suitable for maintaining tracks in straight sections. Rotating wing ballast regulators have a rotating wing blade that allows for efficient maintenance of tracks in curved sections. Articulated rotating wing ballast regulators are more flexible, with an articulated wing blade that can adapt to both straight and curved track sections, providing versatile track maintenance capabilities.</p></p>
