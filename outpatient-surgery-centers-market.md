<p><p>Outpatient Surgery Centers, also known as ambulatory surgery centers (ASCs), are healthcare facilities that offer same-day surgical procedures to patients who do not require an overnight stay. The introduction of robotic-assisted surgeries, advanced imaging technologies, and the integration of electronic health records have improved surgical outcomes and patient experiences.
