Catheters are thin tubes inserted into blood vessels to remove blockages or deliver medication. Hemodynamic Flow Alteration Devices are specialized devices used to redirect blood flow and treat conditions like aneurysms. These devices play a vital role in managing vascular diseases and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
