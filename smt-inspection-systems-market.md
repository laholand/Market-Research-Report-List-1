<p><p>SMT Inspection Systems refer to the equipment used to inspect surface mount technology (SMT) components in electronic manufacturing. There are three main types of SMT Inspection Systems: Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI), Automated Optical Inspection (AOI), and Solder Paste Inspection (SPI). AXI employs X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints, while AOI utilizes cameras and algorithms to detect defects on the surface of components. SPI, on the other hand, inspects the solder paste before components are placed on the board. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of SMT assemblies.</p></p>
