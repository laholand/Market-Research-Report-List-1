<p><p>Body contouring refers to various surgical and non-surgical procedures that aim to shape and sculpt the body. Non-invasive body contouring includes techniques like cryolipolysis (freezing fat cells), laser therapy, or radiofrequency to reduce fat and tighten skin without surgery. Minimally invasive body contouring involves procedures like liposuction or laser-assisted liposuction, which require small incisions and use techniques such as heat or suction to remove excess fat. Invasive body contouring encompasses surgical procedures like abdominoplasty (tummy tuck) or body lifts, which involve larger incisions and more extensive tissue manipulation.</p></p>
