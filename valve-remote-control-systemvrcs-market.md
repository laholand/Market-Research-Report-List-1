There are four main types of VRCS markets: hydraulic, pneumatic, electric, and electro-hydraulic. </p><p>Hydraulic VRCS involves the use of fluid power to control valve operations. Pneumatic VRCS relies on compressed air or gas to operate valves remotely. Electric VRCS uses electrical power to control valve movements. Electro-Hydraulic VRCS combines the features of hydraulic and electric systems, utilizing both fluid power and electrical control to operate valves remotely.</p></p>
