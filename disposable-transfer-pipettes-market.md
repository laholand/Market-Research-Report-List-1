<p><p>Disposable transfer pipettes are commonly used in laboratories and medical settings for transferring small volumes of liquids. There are two main types: disposable graduated transfer pipettes and disposable nongraduated transfer pipettes. Graduated transfer pipettes have markings on the tube that allow for accurate measurement of the liquid volume being transferred. On the other hand, nongraduated transfer pipettes do not have markings and are used when precise measurements are not required. Both types of pipettes are designed for single-use, ensuring hygiene and reducing the risk of cross-contamination in various applications.</p></p>
