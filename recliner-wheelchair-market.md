<p><p>The recliner wheelchair market consists of two main types: electric and mechanical wheelchairs. Electric wheelchairs are powered by an electric motor that enables smooth and convenient movement for individuals with mobility issues. They are controlled by a joystick, making it easier for users to operate them independently. On the other hand, mechanical wheelchairs are manually operated with the help of gears, levers, or wheels. They require the user to exert physical effort to move and navigate. Both types cater to individuals with varying degrees of mobility impairments, offering options that suit different needs.</p></p>
