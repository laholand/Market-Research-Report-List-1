<p><p>Fiber optic cable blowers are used for installing fiber optic cables in various environments. They are available in different types based on the driving mechanism - hydraulically, pneumatically, and electrically. Hydraulically driven blowers use hydraulic power to install cables efficiently. Pneumatically driven blowers use compressed air for cable installation. Electric blowers are powered by electricity and are often used for smaller projects. Each type of blower has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications in the fiber optic cable installation market.</p></p>
