<p><strong>The Electronic Manometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Type</li><li>Simulation Model Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic manometer market offers two types of products: digital type and simulation model type. Digital manometers provide readings in a numerical format, offering accuracy and convenience in measuring pressure. They are commonly used in various industries for precise pressure measurements. On the other hand, simulation model type manometers are designed to mimic pressure conditions for testing and training purposes. They are extensively used in laboratory settings, allowing users to simulate pressure readings without actual pressure variations. Both types cater to different needs in pressure measurement, serving diverse industries and applications.</p></p>
