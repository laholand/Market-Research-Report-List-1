<p><p>The medical tubing market encompasses various types of materials used in the manufacturing of tubing for medical applications. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is a widely used material due to its flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Polyolefin materials offer high purity and chemical resistance. TPE (thermoplastic elastomers) and TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) provide enhanced properties such as flexibility and durability. Silicone tubing is highly biocompatible and resistant to extreme temperatures. Lastly, other materials such as metals and glass are also used in specific medical tubing applications based on their unique properties and requirements.</p></p>
