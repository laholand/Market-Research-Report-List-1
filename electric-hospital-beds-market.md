<p><ul><li>Semi-Electric Beds</li><li>Full Electric Beds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric hospital beds can be categorized into two types: semi-electric beds and full electric beds. Semi-electric beds have electric controls for adjusting the head and foot positions, while manual hand cranks are used to adjust the height. On the other hand, full electric beds offer electric controls for adjusting the head, foot, and height positions, eliminating the need for manual hand cranks. These types of hospital beds are designed to enhance patient comfort, facilitate caregiver tasks, and provide optimal patient care in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
