<p><strong>The Hair Transplant Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hair Transplant Services market offers two main types of procedures: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves removing a strip of hair-bearing skin from the back of the scalp and transplanting it onto the balding areas. On the other hand, FUE involves individually extracting hair follicles from the donor area and implanting them into thinning or balding areas. Both procedures aim to restore hair growth, but FUE is less invasive and leaves minimal scarring, making it more popular among patients.</p></p>
