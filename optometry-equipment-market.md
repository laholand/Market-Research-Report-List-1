The General Examination market offers equipment for overall eye health assessment, such as visual acuity testing and tonometry. Lastly, the Cornea Examination market focuses on instruments used to evaluate the cornea's condition, essential for diagnosing and managing conditions like corneal infections and astigmatism.</p></p>
