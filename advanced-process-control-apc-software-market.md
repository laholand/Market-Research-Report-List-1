<p><p>The Advanced Process Control (APC) Software market comprises various types such as Advanced Regulatory Control (ARC) systems, Multivariable Model Predictive Control (MPC) systems, Inferential Control systems, Sequential Control systems, and Compressor Control systems. ARC systems provide fine-tuning of processes, MPC systems use mathematical models to optimize control strategies, Inferential Control systems use available data to estimate and control process variables, Sequential Control systems control complex processes with a sequence of predefined steps, and Compressor Control systems regulate the operation of compressors for efficient performance.</p></p>
