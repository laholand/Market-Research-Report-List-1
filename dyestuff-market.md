<p><p>Dyestuff market comprises various types of dyes that are used for coloring textiles, paper, plastics, and other materials. Disperse dyes are commonly used for polyester fibers, while reactive dyes are suitable for cellulose-based fibers. Sulfur dyes offer excellent colorfastness on cotton and are widely used in denim production. Vat dyes are known for their deep shades and are used for natural fibers. Acid dyes are primarily used for protein fibers like wool and silk. Other dyes include direct, basic, and pigment dyes, each offering unique properties and applications in the dyestuff market.</p></p>
