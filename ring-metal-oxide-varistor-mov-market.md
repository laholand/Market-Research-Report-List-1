<p><p>The Ring Metal Oxide Varistor (MOV) market can be divided into two main types: Low Voltage (LV) MOV and High Voltage-Medium Voltage (HV-MV) MOV. LV MOVs are designed for protecting low voltage electronic devices and appliances from voltage surges, while HV-MV MOVs are used in industrial and power distribution systems to protect against high voltage spikes. Both types of MOVs provide essential protection against transient overvoltages by diverting excess current away from sensitive equipment, ensuring their longevity and reliability.</p></p>
