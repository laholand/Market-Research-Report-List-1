These mixtures are then used for various construction projects such as roads, highways, airports, and parking lots.</p><p>The market for asphalt batch plants is witnessing steady growth due to increasing infrastructure development activities across the globe. Medium batch plant has a capacity between 40 and 160 tons per hour, designed for mid-sized construction projects. Large batch plant, with a capacity of more than 160 tons per hour, is suitable for large-scale construction projects, such as highways and airports.
