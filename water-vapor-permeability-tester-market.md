<p><p>The water vapor permeability tester market is segmented into four types. The Gravimetric Method, also known as the Cup Method, measures the weight change of a cup containing a sample to determine water vapor permeability. The Electrolytic Detection Sensor Method detects water vapor using a sensor that measures electrical conductivity. The Infrared Detection Sensor Method uses an infrared sensor to detect water vapor levels. The Humidity Detection Sensor Method employs a humidity sensor to measure the moisture content. These methods provide different ways to accurately measure water vapor permeability in various materials.</p></p>
