<p><p>The industrial medium-voltage cables market is categorized into various types. XLPE cables refer to cross-linked polyethylene cables, typically used for transmitting power in underground networks due to their high electrical and thermal resistance. MI cables, also known as mineral insulated cables, are fire-resistant and often employed in buildings and industrial applications. EPR cables are ethylene propylene rubber cables, which offer excellent resistance to heat, oils, and chemicals, making them suitable for industrial environments. Each type serves specific purposes in the industrial medium-voltage cables market.</p></p>
