The types of LDHI include Anti-Agglomerate (AA) Inhibitors, which prevent the agglomeration of gas hydrate particles, Kinetic Hydrate Inhibitors (KHI), which slow down the rate of hydrate formation, and Others, which refer to different types of inhibitors with unique mechanisms of action. Each type of LDHI serves a specific purpose in inhibiting gas hydrate formation in pipelines and equipment.</p></p>
