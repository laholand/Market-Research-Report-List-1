<p><p>Containment Fill Stations (CFS) are used in the compressed gas industry to fill high-pressure breathing air cylinders. There are two main types of CFS: stationary and mobile. Stationary CFS are permanently installed at a fixed location, typically in fire stations or dive shops. They are designed to provide a centralized and convenient solution for filling cylinders. On the other hand, mobile CFS are portable units that can be transported to different locations, making them ideal for remote or temporary sites such as construction sites or emergency response teams. Both types serve the same purpose but cater to different needs and contexts.</p></p>
