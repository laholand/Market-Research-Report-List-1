<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The on-site hydrogen generator market includes two main types: traditional alkaline electrolyser and PEM electrolyser. Traditional alkaline electrolyser produces hydrogen through an electrochemical reaction using a liquid alkaline electrolyte. It is widely used due to its low cost and high efficiency. PEM electrolyser, on the other hand, utilizes a solid polymer membrane to separate the hydrogen and oxygen gases during electrolysis. This type is preferred in applications that require compactness, fast response time, and high purity hydrogen production. Both types play a crucial role in on-site hydrogen generation for various industries.</p></p>
