<p><p>The market for large polishing machines can be divided into two types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type large polishing machines are designed for processing a fixed number of workpieces in a single batch. They are suitable for small-scale production or when precise control over each piece is required. Continuous type large polishing machines, on the other hand, are designed for continuous processing of workpieces without the need for individual batch processing. They are ideal for large-scale production or when a high volume of workpieces needs to be polished.</p></p>
