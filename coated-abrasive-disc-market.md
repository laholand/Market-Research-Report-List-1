<p><strong>The Coated Abrasive Disc Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Abrasive</li><li>Super Abrasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coated abrasive discs are widely used in various industries for grinding, polishing, and finishing applications. The market for these discs can be categorized into two main types: conventional abrasive and super abrasive. Conventional abrasive discs are made from materials like aluminum oxide or silicon carbide and are suitable for general-purpose grinding and smoothing tasks. On the other hand, super abrasive discs, such as diamond or cubic boron nitride (CBN) discs, offer exceptional durability, precision, and cutting power, making them ideal for high-precision grinding and materials like concrete, stone, or glass. These two types cater to different requirements and have distinct advantages within the coated abrasive disc market.</p></p>
