<p><p>Periodontal probes are dental instruments used to measure the depth of periodontal pockets around teeth. There are two main types of periodontal probes based on the number of working ends: single-ended and double-ended probes. Single-ended probes have one working end with a rounded tip for measuring pocket depths. Double-ended probes have two working ends, one with a rounded tip and the other with a flat tip, allowing for additional functionalities such as detecting tooth mobility. These different probe types cater to specific dental examination needs.</p></p>
