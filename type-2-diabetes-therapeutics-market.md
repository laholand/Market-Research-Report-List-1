Biguanides reduce glucose production by the liver and improve insulin sensitivity. Insulins are injected to regulate blood sugar levels. SGLT-2 inhibitors work by eliminating excess glucose through urine. α-Glucosidase inhibitors slow down carbohydrate absorption in the gut. Lastly, other therapeutic options include combination drugs or natural remedies. These medications aim to control blood sugar levels, improve insulin response, and prevent diabetes-related complications.</p></p>
