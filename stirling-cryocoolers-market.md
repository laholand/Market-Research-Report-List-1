<p><p>The Stirling cryocoolers market is divided into two main types: linear cryocoolers and rotary cryocoolers. Linear cryocoolers operate by converting mechanical input into linear oscillating motion, which is then converted into cooling power through the Stirling cycle. Rotary cryocoolers, on the other hand, utilize rotating mechanisms to produce cooling effects. Both types are used in various applications, such as cooling infrared sensors, medical equipment, and electronics. They play a crucial role in providing reliable and efficient cryogenic cooling solutions.</p></p>
