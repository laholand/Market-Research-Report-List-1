<p><p>The transponder market is divided into various types, including C-band, Ku-band, Ka-band, and others. C-band transponders operate at a frequency range of 4 to 8 gigahertz (GHz), offering reliable communication in adverse weather conditions. Ku-band transponders operate at a frequency range of 12 to 18 GHz, providing higher bandwidth and enhanced communication capabilities. Ka-band transponders operate at a frequency range of 26.5 to 40 GHz, offering even higher bandwidth and enabling high-speed data transmission. Other types of transponders include L-band, S-band, and X-band, each serving different communication needs.</p></p>
