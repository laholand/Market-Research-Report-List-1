<p><p>Worm Screw Jacks market includes three different types: Translating Screw Jacks, Trapezoidal Screw Jacks, and Rotating Screw Jacks. Translating Screw Jacks are used to provide linear motion by converting the rotary motion of the input shaft into linear motion of the lifting screw. Trapezoidal Screw Jacks are designed with a trapezoidal thread form to enable higher load capacities and ensure self-locking capability. Rotating Screw Jacks are suitable for applications requiring rotational motion, as they convert the rotary motion of the input shaft into rotational motion of the lifting screw.</p></p>
