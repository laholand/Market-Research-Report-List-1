<p><p>A Blast Regenerative Adsorption Dryer is a type of air dryer that removes moisture from compressed air using a process called adsorption. This type of dryer is commonly used in industries where the presence of moisture can cause damage to equipment or adversely affect the quality of the final product.</p><p>The adsorption process involves passing the compressed air through a desiccant material, such as activated alumina or molecular sieve, which adsorbs the moisture molecules. The dryer then regenerates the desiccant by directing a portion of the dried air through it at low pressure and temperature, effectively stripping off the adsorbed moisture. This regenerated air is then expelled to the atmosphere. The cycle is repeated continuously to ensure a continuous supply of dry air.</p><p>The Blast Regenerative Adsorption Dryer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.
