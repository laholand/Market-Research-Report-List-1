<p><p>The residential food dehydrator market offers two popular types: stacked trays and slide-in trays. Stacked tray dehydrators feature multiple trays that are stacked on top of each other. This design allows for efficient dehydration of different food items all at once. On the other hand, slide-in tray dehydrators have trays that slide into the machine horizontally. Both types cater to home users looking to preserve and dry different types of food at their convenience.</p></p>
