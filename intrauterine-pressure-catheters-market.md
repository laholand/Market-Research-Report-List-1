<p><p>The Intrauterine Pressure Catheters market encompasses various types of products. Catheters are flexible tubes inserted into the uterus to accurately measure the pressure during labor. Cables/Transducers transmit the pressure signals from the catheter to the monitoring equipment. Monitoring Equipment records and displays the intrauterine pressure readings. Disposables include single-use catheters and transducers that are discarded after use. These different components work together to provide healthcare professionals with important information about uterine contractions and pressure, aiding in the management of labor and delivery.</p></p>
