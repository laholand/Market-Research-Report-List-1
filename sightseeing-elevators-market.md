<p><p>Sightseeing elevators refer to specialized lifts designed to provide scenic views to passengers while transporting them between different floors or levels of a building. There are three main types of sightseeing elevators in the market: platform lifts, cabin lifts, and other lifts. Platform lifts feature an open or glass platform, allowing passengers to have an unobstructed view during their ride. Cabin lifts, on the other hand, have glass walls and ceiling, providing panoramic views from inside the elevator. Lastly, the "other lifts" category includes unique designs and variations that also emphasize scenic views.</p></p>
