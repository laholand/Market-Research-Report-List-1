<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aircraft Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reduction Gearbox (RGB)</li><li>Accessory Gearbox (AGB)</li><li>Actuation Gearbox</li><li>Tail Rotor Gearbox</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft gearbox market comprises various types of gearboxes used in different components of an aircraft. Reduction Gearbox (RGB) is responsible for reducing the engine's rotation speed to drive the propeller efficiently. Accessory Gearbox (AGB) assists in powering and controlling aircraft accessories like generators and pumps. Actuation Gearbox plays a vital role in controlling different aircraft systems, such as landing gear, flaps, and hydraulic pumps. Tail Rotor Gearbox aids in transmitting power from the main rotor to the tail rotor. Lastly, the market also includes other gearboxes used in different aircraft applications.</p></p>
