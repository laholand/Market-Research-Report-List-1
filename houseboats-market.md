<p><p>Houseboats can be categorized into two market types based on their motor types: outboard motor type and inboard motor type. Outboard motor houseboats are equipped with motors that are located outside the boat, typically attached to the transom. These types of houseboats are known for their maneuverability, ease of maintenance, and ability to operate in shallow waters. In contrast, inboard motor houseboats have engines situated inside the boat's hull. These types of houseboats offer more power, better fuel efficiency, and are generally larger in size compared to outboard motor houseboats.</p></p>
